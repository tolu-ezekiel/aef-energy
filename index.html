About Us


Who we are

A Nigerian based company with its head office in Lagos.

We partner with world-class expertise from within the oil, gas and extractive industries to provide cutting-edge asset management solutions covering the entire oil and gas value chain.

Vision

To become Africa's preferred energy support services company, driving efficiency through innovation and technology.

Our aim

To drive towards more profitable assets management through business focused technical solutions and the application of international standards & best practice.
